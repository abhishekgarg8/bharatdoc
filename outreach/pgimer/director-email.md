- Doctor-reviewed summaries only
- Clear AI-assisted disclaimer and audit trail
- Pilot launch subject to PGIMER IEC/IRB, IT/security, data-protection, department, and patient-consent approvals
- Phase-one exclusions: no emergency care, no autonomous diagnosis, no prescribing, no HIS/EHR writeback, and no high-risk medico-legal use unless PGIMER approves
- Success measured by time saved, note quality, doctor satisfaction, transcription success, and PDF generation reliability
