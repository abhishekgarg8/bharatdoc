## Positioning

Lead with controlled evaluation under PGIMER governance:

- Doctor-reviewed AI documentation.
- No autonomous diagnosis.
- No prescribing.
- No HIS/EHR writeback during phase one.
- Pilot launch subject to PGIMER IEC/IRB, IT/security, data-protection, department, and patient-consent approvals.

## Follow-Up Ask

Request a 20-minute meeting with nominated representatives from:

- Clinical leadership
- Hospital administration
- IT/security
- Legal/data-protection
- IEC/ethics
- One candidate OPD department
