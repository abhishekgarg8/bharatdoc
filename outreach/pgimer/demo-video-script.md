# PGIMER Director Demo Video

Target length: 58-60 seconds  
Audience: Director, PGIMER Chandigarh, plus nominated clinical/admin/IT reviewers  
Tone: institutional, calm, practical, doctor-controlled

## Core Message

BharatDoc helps PGIMER evaluate doctor-reviewed AI documentation in a controlled OPD pilot, subject to PGIMER governance approvals and without changing existing hospital systems.

## Storyboard

| Time | Visual | Caption / voiceover |
| ---: | --- | --- |
| 0-6s | Busy teaching-hospital OPD visual, doctors reviewing files | PGIMER handles complex, high-volume care. Documentation should support doctors, not slow them down. |
| 6-13s | BharatDoc pilot screen: 30-day controlled pilot | Start narrow: 1 to 3 OPD teams, 10 to 20 doctors or residents, subject to PGIMER approvals, no HIS integration. |
| 13-23s | Phone recording consultation audio after consent workflow | A doctor records the consultation using a PGIMER-approved consent and data-handling process. |
| 23-34s | Transcript becomes structured sections | BharatDoc drafts history, findings, assessment, treatment, and follow-up sections from the consultation. |
| 34-45s | Doctor edits summary and taps save | AI drafts. Doctors decide. No note is finalized until the clinician reviews and edits it. |
| 45-53s | Patient-ID PDF and audit/data-flow graphic | The final Patient-ID PDF is generated with an AI-assisted disclaimer and auditable workflow. |
| 53-60s | Pilot dashboard metrics | PGIMER can measure time saved, note quality, edit rate, transcription success, and doctor satisfaction. |

## On-Screen Text Rules

- Use "doctor-reviewed" and "controlled pilot".
- Avoid "diagnosis", "autonomous", "replacement", "fully automatic", or "guaranteed".
- Show "No HIS integration during pilot" clearly.
- Show "doctor reviews before saving" clearly.
- Show "subject to PGIMER IEC/IRB, IT/security, data-protection, and patient-consent approvals" where space allows.
- Use captions so the video works without audio.
